Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental services

Booking & Reservations

You can book a vehicle in three simple ways:

  • Click "Book Now" on any vehicle page and fill the form
  • WhatsApp us at +91 99753 56697 with your requirements
  • Call us directly at +91 99753 56697

The minimum rental duration is 24 hours. However, we offer flexible hourly bookings upon request for special cases. Contact us for more details.

Yes! We accept bookings up to 6 months in advance. Early booking gives you better rates and vehicle availability. A 25% advance deposit is required to confirm your booking.

Pricing & Payment

The rental price includes:

  • Vehicle hire charges
  • Comprehensive insurance (for damage liability up to security deposit)
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Complimentary mobile phone charger
  • Free hotel/airport pickup and drop

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, PayTM)
  • Cash payment at pickup

Yes, a security deposit is required at the time of rental. The amount varies by vehicle type (typically ₹3,000 to ₹10,000). This is refundable after the rental period, subject to vehicle condition inspection.

Requirements & Documentation

You need to provide:

  • Valid Driving License (with current address)
  • Passport or Aadhar Card (for verification)
  • Credit/Debit Card (for security deposit)
  • Hotel address or contact details during rental period

The minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21 years. Your driving license must have been valid for at least 1 year. Additional deposit may apply for drivers under 25 years.

Yes! International visitors can rent vehicles with a valid International Driving Permit (IDP) or valid driving license in English. A passport is required for identification.

Pickup & Drop-off

Standard pickup hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. However, we provide 24/7 on-demand pickup for airport arrivals and other emergencies. An additional charge of ₹500-1000 may apply for out-of-hours pickup.

Absolutely! Free airport and hotel pickup/drop is included for all rentals within Goa. Our staff will meet you with a name board at the airport.

Late returns are charged as follows:

  • Up to 2 hours late: ₹500
  • 2-4 hours late: ₹1,000 + hourly charges
  • Beyond 4 hours: Full day rental charges apply

Driving & Insurance

All rentals include comprehensive insurance covering:

  • Third-party liability protection
  • Damage coverage (up to security deposit amount)
  • Theft protection
  • Personal accident insurance for driver

Yes, you can add one additional driver for an extra charge of ₹300-500 per day. The additional driver must have a valid driving license and meet our age requirements.

In case of an accident:

  • Call our 24/7 emergency number immediately
  • Do not move the vehicle unless it's causing obstruction
  • Take photos/videos of the damage and scene
  • Get details of other vehicles/witnesses involved
  • Contact local police if required
  • Inform us within 24 hours

Fuel & Maintenance

Yes, all vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel. You must return the vehicle with a full tank. If fuel is missing, you'll be charged at current market rates plus a 10% service charge.

If your rental vehicle breaks down, call us immediately. We provide 24/7 roadside assistance including:

  • Free towing service
  • Vehicle replacement if required
  • No extra charges for mechanical breakdowns

Yes, all routine maintenance is covered including oil changes, tire rotations, and filter replacements. We ensure all vehicles are well-maintained and serviced regularly.

Cancellation & Refunds

Our cancellation policy:

  • 7+ days before rental: Full refund
  • 3-7 days before: 50% refund
  • 1-3 days before: 25% refund
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund

Your security deposit will be refunded within 5-7 business days after return, subject to vehicle condition inspection. Any damages beyond normal wear and tear will be deducted from the deposit.
