FAQ

Maruti Baleno Rental FAQs

Why is Baleno the cheapest rental option?
The Baleno is affordable to operate due to excellent fuel efficiency (23 km/l) and lower maintenance costs. We pass these savings on to customers, making it our most budget-friendly option.
Is the Baleno suitable for highway driving?
Yes! The Baleno is highway-capable with good acceleration and stability. It's perfect for Goa to neighboring state trips. Its fuel efficiency makes long drives economical.
Does Baleno have automatic transmission available?
Yes, we offer both manual and automatic Baleno variants. Automatic transmission is available at a slightly higher daily rate. Check WhatsApp for current availability.
What makes Baleno different from i20?
Both are excellent hatchbacks. Baleno offers better value & fuel efficiency, while i20 provides more features. Both seat 5 adults comfortably. Choose based on your budget & preferences.
