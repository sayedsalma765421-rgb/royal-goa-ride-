Booking & Cancellation Policy

Booking Confirmation

All bookings are confirmed via WhatsApp or phone call. A confirmation message with car details, pickup time, and driver contact information will be sent immediately. Payment can be made at pickup or via online transfer. A security deposit is required for all bookings.

Cancellation Terms

48+ hours before: Full refund of advance payment. 24-48 hours: 25% cancellation charge. Less than 24 hours: 50% cancellation charge. No-show: 100% charge. For long-term rentals (30+ days), different terms apply with 7-day notice for free cancellation.

Insurance Coverage & Protection

Comprehensive coverage included in all rentals

Third-Party Liability

Covers legal liability for bodily injury or damage to third-party vehicles or property. This is mandatory by law in India and included in all rentals. Minimum coverage ₹1 crore.

Own-Damage Protection

Covers damage to the rental vehicle from accidents, collisions, or theft. Deductible of ₹5,000-₹10,000 applies depending on vehicle and driving conditions. Claims processed within 7 days.

Zero Depreciation Coverage

Optional add-on (₹200-₹300/day) that eliminates depreciation charges on repairs. Recommended for peace of mind. No claim deductible with this coverage.

Personal Accident Cover

Optional add-on covering accidental death or permanent disability of the driver. Coverage up to ₹10 lakhs. Premium ₹100-₹150/day depending on coverage level.

Baggage & Personal Effects

Coverage for baggage and personal belongings left in the vehicle. Limited coverage up to ₹50,000 for theft or damage. Valuables must be reported before rental.

Roadside Assistance

24/7 breakdown support including tire puncture, battery assistance, fuel delivery, and mechanical help. Free for basic services; major repairs charged at cost. Available across Goa.

What's NOT Covered

Insurance does not cover damage from intentional misuse, rash driving, off-road damage, collision under influence, speeding violation fines, parking violations, or use for commercial purposes. Rental agreement violations void all coverage.

Damage Policy & Liability

Minor Damage Charges

  • • Scratches/paint damage: ₹2,000-₹5,000
  • • Dented panels: ₹3,000-₹8,000
  • • Broken mirrors: ₹2,000-₹3,000
  • • Cracked windows: ₹3,000-₹5,000
  • • Interior stains: ₹1,000-₹2,000
  • • Missing parts: Actual replacement cost

Major Damage & Accidents

  • • Engine damage: 50% of vehicle value
  • • Transmission damage: 40% of vehicle value
  • • Total loss: Full vehicle value
  • • Electrical damage: ₹5,000-₹15,000
  • • Suspension/chassis damage: ₹8,000-₹20,000
  • • Frame damage: 30% of vehicle value

Accident Protocol

In case of an accident, immediately contact our 24/7 helpline at +91 99753 56697. Do not admit fault or negotiate with third parties. Take photographs of all damage, get police report (FIR) if injuries or major damage, and provide all documents within 24 hours. We handle insurance claims and third-party negotiations.

Security Deposit Details

Amount: ₹5,000 for economy cars, ₹10,000 for SUVs, ₹15,000 for premium vehicles. Format: Cash or credit card hold (not deducted). Refund: Full refund within 7 days of return after damage inspection. Non-refundable: Damage charges, traffic violations, parking fines, and extra kilometer charges are deducted from deposit.

Additional Charges & Fees

Extra Kilometers

Daily rentals include 150 km/day. Extra kilometers charged at ₹8/km for economy cars, ₹10/km for SUVs, ₹12/km for premium vehicles. Monthly rentals include unlimited kilometers.

Late Return Charge

Checkout until 12 PM is free. After noon, ₹300/hour for first 4 hours (economy), ₹400/hour (SUV), ₹500/hour (premium). Full day charge applies after 4 hours. Plan accordingly for airport returns.

Traffic Violations & Fines

You are responsible for all traffic violations, parking fines, and toll charges incurred during rental. Fines are deducted from security deposit or charged separately. Speed camera fines processed within 30 days.

Fuel Charges

Vehicles are provided with full tank. Return with full tank to avoid fuel charge. If returned with less fuel, ₹25/liter will be charged. Top-up fuel available at our office or partner pumps across Goa.

Early Return Credit

Return car before rental period ends = pro-rata refund. For example, returning 5 days early on a 10-day rental gets 50% of the daily rate refunded. No penalties for early return.

Driver Change Fee

Additional driver can be added for free. Name and license details must be provided at booking. Driver change during rental costs ₹500 per change (maximum 2 changes included).

Vehicle Condition

Upon pickup, inspect the vehicle thoroughly and photograph any existing damage. Report damage to us via WhatsApp before leaving the pickup location. We'll provide a pre-rental inspection form listing all existing marks, scratches, or damage. Return the vehicle in the same condition. Smoke, food, or pet odors incur ₹2,000-₹5,000 cleaning charges.
