Why self-drive works well in South Goa

It adds flexibility to slower-paced travel and coastal exploration.

South Goa is ideal for travelers who want relaxed beach mornings and scenic drives through quieter towns. Having your own vehicle allows you to visit multiple beach pockets, explore local restaurants, and visit nearby landmarks at times that suit you. It is also convenient for travelers staying outside the main beach belt and wanting direct access to their accommodations.
